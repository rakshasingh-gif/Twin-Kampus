# Twin Campus Initiative – IIT Kanpur

## Overview

The Twin Campus Initiative is a Digital Twin project undertaken under the **DESCON Society, IIT Kanpur**. The project focuses on developing accurate 3D digital models of IIT Kanpur's academic buildings and residential halls to create a virtual representation of the campus for visualization and future smart-campus applications.

## Objectives

* Develop high-fidelity 3D models of campus infrastructure.
* Create scalable digital assets for Digital Twin applications.
* Improve campus visualization and virtual infrastructure planning.

## My Contributions

* Designed detailed 3D models of multiple academic buildings and residential halls.
* Created engineering models using "Fusion 360" and "Autodesk Inventor".
* Applied parametric design and feature-based CAD modeling techniques to ensure structural accuracy.
* Collaborated with team members to maintain geometric consistency and optimize model complexity.
* Generated engineering drawings, sectional views, and assembly layouts for documentation.
